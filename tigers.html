Tigers

Some fun tiger facts for you to tell your friends!

  • There are more tigers privately owned by U.S. citizens than in the wild worldwide
  • Tigers stripes are also found on their skin
  • Their stripes are like human fingerprints, unique to each one
  • Tigers are the only predators known to prey on adult bears

There are no wild tigers in Africa, only in Asia

  • The Bengal Tiger is the national animal of India
  • Less than 10% of tiger hunts end successfully

  • Tigers can eat up to 60 pounds of meat in a single night
